Mutombo Esdras

The Democratic Republic of the Congo
Biography collected and written by Divine Irakoze




Biography

Mutombo Esdras is originally from the The Democratic Republic of the Congo, but is currently living in Malawi as a refugee. He is the first born in a family of six children. He is a Certified Software Developer with three years of experience in software development and User Interface design.

Apart from that, Mutombo is also a poet. He spends most of his spare time writing poetry. Otherwise, he dedicates his time working on many different kinds of projects. He is a young man with a strong and vivid imagination. He believes everything is possible and that he can always achieve more and contribute to making the world a better place. Currently, he volunteers as a receptionist with a Brazilian non-profit organization in Dzaleka called Fraternity Without Borders, which provides education to poor children.

His future plan is to become an advanced computer scientist by finishing at university and launching his own start-ups that will help lots of vulnerable people across the globe with many different helpful services. He also plans to publish a poetry anthology in the near future, as poetry is his passion.

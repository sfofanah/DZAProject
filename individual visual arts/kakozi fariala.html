Biography


Kakozi Fariala is a young artist. Born in 1997, he left his country in 2018 due to war. He currently lives with his family in the Dzaleka refugee camp where he paints to earn a living. He draws and paints. He received training from his fellow artists in the camp. Unfortunately, he has difficulty finding adequate materials to do his work. Prices are very low on the camp market, which significantly minimizes the profit he can make.
